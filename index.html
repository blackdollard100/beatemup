Beat 'em up!

Use the arrow keys and space bar on your keyboard to move and fight.

This project by Simon Sablowski is a HTML5 and JavaScript-based tribute to Mortal Kombat,
a classic beat 'em up game developed by NetherRealm Studios, formerly Midway Games Chicago.
All graphics, characters and related elements are taken from the original game, trademarks and
